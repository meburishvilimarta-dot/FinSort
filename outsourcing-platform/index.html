Why route it through Georgia

Georgia sits between the markets you're hiring from and the ones you sell to.

For the Georgian company

  • Same-day time overlap with both Delhi and Manila, without either side working a night shift.
  • IT-friendly business regimes like Small Business and Virtual Zone status make structuring an export-services arrangement straightforward.
  • Fast company setup — Georgian entities can typically be registered in about a day, with unrestricted foreign-currency accounts.
  • One contract, one invoice instead of managing payroll compliance in two foreign countries yourself.

Tax and regulatory specifics change — confirm current rules with a Georgian accountant or attorney before structuring anything.

For the talent

  • Deep, mature talent pools — India's software and data engineering base and the Philippines' decades of BPO experience are two of the most established outsourcing markets in the world.
  • Reliable pay cycles routed through SilkBridge, not dependent on a single client's cash flow.
  • Direct client relationships with Georgian businesses, not resold through multiple layers of agencies.
  • Room to grow from a single contract into an ongoing, long-term working relationship.
